About Me

Hello, my name is Tatsu, and I am studying in the IMD program at Algonquin College. I'm from the city of Pokemon in Japan. So, when I was a kid, I was crazy about Pokemon games. Now I've been really into taking pictures. Likewise, I like wild squirrels when shooting in Canada, for their bouncing movement is so cute and feels like a real Pokemon.

Finally, my future goal is to be a worldwide creator who can generate what everyone knows about like Pokemon. To that end, I think that the most important issues are to obtain a free and creative work style as a short-term goal and to create an environment where I can freely create what I want to make independently as a creator.
